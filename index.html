The Dog. The Myth. The Legend.

Ryder is one of the cutest dogs on the planet. He was a rescue: found on the side of a back country road in Jackson County, Ohio. It took weeks to befriend him, but making friends was the best thing we could have done for him. He loves to snuggle and hide under blankets, and get into the garbage. Car rides are not his favorite thing, but he enjoys going for long walks with his people. He loves to pose for the camera and eats up any attention he's given.
